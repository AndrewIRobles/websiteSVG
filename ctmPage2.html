Corporate Travel Management FAQ

Quick answers stakeholders ask before switching or scaling a travel program.

What does a corporate travel management company do?
A corporate travel management company helps organizations plan, book, and manage business travel with traveler support, policy workflows, supplier strategy, and reporting.
How do you support travelers during disruptions?
Balboa supports itinerary changes and cancellations, helps travelers rebook quickly, and escalates time-critical travel issues through service-first support and duty of care options.
Can you enforce travel policy and approvals?
Yes. Policy-aligned workflows, approval routing, and exception handling can be implemented to balance compliance with traveler experience.
Do you support global travel programs?
Yes. Balboa supports multi-location and international travel programs with consolidated reporting and consistent servicing.
What reporting do travel managers and finance teams get?
Custom reporting across spend, compliance, unused tickets, and stakeholder-specific views (including sustainability reporting support when needed).
Do you integrate with expense platforms?
Balboa supports common expense platforms and corporate ERP configurations, including (but not limited to) Chrome River and PeopleSoft.
What makes Balboa different?
Service-first support backed by modern technology—designed for real-world program complexity, not just bookings.
How do we get started?
We’ll review your current program, goals, traveler profile, and systems, then map a rollout plan aligned to your policy and reporting needs.
