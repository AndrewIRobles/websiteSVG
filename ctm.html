Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we implement Balboa?

Implementation timelines vary based on program complexity, but most enterprise clients are fully operational within 60-90 days. We offer expedited onboarding for urgent needs, with some clients going live in as little as 30 days.

What expense systems do you integrate with?

Balboa integrates with enterprise expense and finance ecosystems, including Chrome River, Oracle-based expense systems, and bespoke internal solutions. Our API-first architecture supports secure integration via API or SFTP, allowing us to connect with a wide range of platforms and workflows across the enterprise stack.

How do you handle data security and compliance?

We maintain SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR, and PCI DSS compliance. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with role-based access controls and comprehensive audit trails. We undergo annual third-party security assessments and can support custom compliance requirements.

What support levels are available for enterprise clients?

All enterprise clients receive 24/7 global support with dedicated account management. We offer tiered service levels including priority routing for time-critical requests, executive escalation protocols, and proactive disruption monitoring.

What services does Balboa Travel provide?

Balboa partners with organizations to design, manage, and continuously improve their corporate travel programs, while also supporting meetings and incentive travel and leisure experiences. Our core services span travel operations, strategic consulting, data and analytics, technology integration, and traveler support—combining personalized service with scalable, enterprise-grade technology.
